Integration test for the BinSelector residue selector.  This test imports a mixed pose and designs
the positive-phi helical regions to DALA, the negative-phi helical regions to ALA, the positive-phi
sheet regions to DVAL, and the negative-phi sheet regions to VAL.

Author: Vikram K. Mulligan, Ph.D., Baker laboratory, University of Washington (vmullig@uw.edu).
